How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles.
When two identical tiles touch, they merge and become the next level of tile. Get a tile to the eleventh level ("2048") to win.

Hit "=" to preview the tiles. Move a tile to continue.
Hit "-" to hide/unhide the background image.
Hit TAB to hide the tiles and view the full background image. Hit TAB again to show the tiles again.

All of the images you see have been supplied by our users. If there is a problem with anything, please report it to .

This Build-Your-Own 2048 game is based on an original program by Gabriele Cirulli's MIT-licensed 2048, which was based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
